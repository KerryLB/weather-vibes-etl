# ============================================
#   YOUR WEATHER VIBE GUIDE — LOUISVILLE, KY
# ============================================
# Not just a forecast. A whole mood.
# All vibes powered by real weather data.
# ============================================


⛈️ DRAMATIC WEATHER ERA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       The sky is not okay and it wants you to know it. Rain plus
                wind means the weather is actively having a crisis. Hold onto
                your umbrella and your dignity.
Parameters:     Heavy rain (>= 0.50 in) and wind >= 25 mph, OR meaningful
                rain and wind >= 25 mph.
The fit:        Waterproof shell jacket, rain boots, dark jeans.
                Prepared but make it fashion.
Accessory:      Umbrella and a waterproof bag. ☂️


🌧️ LO-FI CHILL DAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       The rain showed up but forgot to bring the drama. It is the
                kind of drizzly day that was made for staying in or pretending
                you enjoy walking in light rain.
Parameters:     Meaningful rain (precip >= 0.10 in or precip hours >= 2),
                wind < 25 mph, and max temp < 86F.
The fit:        Oversized hoodie, joggers, white sneakers.
                Cozy with low effort.
Accessory:      Headphones and a warm drink. 🎧



🌧️🥵 HOT MESS EXPRESS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       Hot, humid, and raining. Nobody asked for this combination
                and yet here we are. You will be damp from both directions
                and there is nothing you can do about it.
Parameters:     Meaningful rain (precip >= 0.10 in or precip hours >= 2),
                wind < 25 mph, and max temp >= 86F.
The fit:        Lightweight shorts, a moisture-wicking tee, waterproof sandals.
                There is no winning outfit today, just damage control.
Accessory:      A compact umbrella and the acceptance that your hair is not
                going to cooperate. ☔


💨 UNHINGED WIND DAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       Not a cloud in sight but the wind has chosen chaos. Dry and
                gusty with zero apology. Your hair is on its own today.
Parameters:     No meaningful rain and wind >= 25 mph.
The fit:        Fitted athletic wear, windbreaker, baseball cap pulled low.
                Aerodynamic on purpose.
Accessory:      Cap and lip balm. 🧢


🥵 OFFENSIVELY HOT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       The sun is too close and too confident. Hot, dry, and
                absolutely relentless. Hydration is not optional.
Parameters:     Max temp >= 90F, no meaningful rain, wind < 25 mph.
The fit:        Linen shorts, breathable tee, sandals, sunglasses.
                Surrender to the heat gracefully.
Accessory:      Sunglasses and SPF 50. 🕶️


🫠 ALMOST TOO MUCH
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       Warm enough to question every decision you made getting
                dressed. Not quite suffering but you are aware of the sun
                at all times.
Parameters:     Max temp 86 to 89F, no meaningful rain, wind < 25 mph.
The fit:        Lightweight shorts, a loose breathable tee, slip-on sneakers.
                Minimal layers and maximum airflow.
Accessory:      Water bottle and some ice cream $$ just in case. 💧


🥶 JUST LET IT GO DAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       Cold and blustery with something to prove. This is the weather
                that makes you question why you live here and then immediately
                remember the food scene.
Parameters:     Max temp <= 35F, no meaningful rain, wind >= 20 mph.
The fit:        Puffer jacket, thermal underlayer, beanie, boots.
                Full armor.
Accessory:      Gloves and hand warmers. 🧤


😎 MAIN CHARACTER ENERGY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       The weather is doing everything right and so are you. Warm,
                dry, breezy enough to feel alive but not enough to ruin
                anything. Go outside.
Parameters:     Max temp 72 to 85F, no meaningful rain, wind < 15 mph.
The fit:        Light wash jeans, tucked-in tee, clean sneakers.
                You look good and you know it.
Accessory:      Fresh kicks and your favorite playlist. 🎵


🌤️ SOFT LAUNCH DAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       Pleasant without being showy about it. The kind of day that
                eases you in gently and asks very little of you.
                A solid B plus weather day.
Parameters:     Max temp 60 to 71F, no meaningful rain, wind < 15 mph.
The fit:        Relaxed trousers or straight-leg pants, a light crewneck,
                clean shoes. Quietly put together.
Accessory:      A light jacket tied around the waist just in case. 🧥


🧣 COZY BLANKET DAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       Cool, calm, and quietly delightful. The air has a bite but
                nothing a good layer cannot fix. Soup weather. Thermos weather.
                Good book weather.
Parameters:     Max temp 35 to 50F, no meaningful rain, wind < 15 mph.
The fit:        Fleece pullover, corduroy pants, chunky knit beanie.
                Warm without trying too hard.
Accessory:      Scarf and a thermos. 🧣


😶 EXISTENTIAL MEH
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
The vibe:       Not bad, not good, just kind of... there. Temps are middling,
                wind is meh, sky has no strong opinions.
Parameters:     Fallback. Conditions do not match any other vibe category.
The fit:        Whatever is clean. Honestly a hoodie and sweatpants. No one
                including the weather has strong feelings today.
Accessory:      A bag because you probably have somewhere to be. 🎒
